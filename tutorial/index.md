# Tutorial

> **IMPORTANT**  
> This resource assumes that you already have fundamentals in *Lua* and *LÖVE (Love2D)*.
> If you don't, here are some great resources to get started:
> - [Getting started with LÖVE (Love2D)](https://love2d.org/wiki/Getting_Started)
> - [LÖVE (Love2D) tutorial aggregation](https://love2d.org/wiki/Category:Tutorials)
>   - [How to LÖVE (LÖVE 11.3)](http://sheepolution.com/learn/book/contents)
>   - [CS50's Introduction to Game Development (LÖVE 0.10.2)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GfwpRU0cT10)
>
> We recommend you to write a few quick games without a framework first to understand the basics of your tool (*LÖVE*) and only *then* move on to frameworks such as nova.  
> We also choose to assume you have fundamentals in *shells* (specifically `bash`) to use and communicate with your development tools.
